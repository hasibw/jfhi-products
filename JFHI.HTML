Donkey Milk Whitening Cream

120ml / 120g J6076 / J6075 216 units/carton

Product Overview

The JFHI® Donkey Milk Whitening Cream in our signature blue packaging represents the pinnacle of brightening skincare technology. This luxurious formulation harnesses the natural brightening properties of pure donkey milk, which has been scientifically proven to contain high levels of lysozyme and lactoferrin—compounds that naturally inhibit melanin production while promoting cellular renewal.

Each 120ml jar contains a concentrated blend of active ingredients designed to target hyperpigmentation, dark spots, and uneven skin tone. The unique blue packaging isn't merely aesthetic; the opaque glass protects the sensitive vitamin C and retinol derivatives from light degradation, ensuring maximum potency throughout the product's lifecycle.

Key Benefits

Our clinical studies demonstrate visible results within 14 days of consistent use. The cream works on multiple levels: first, the alpha-hydroxy acids (AHAs) naturally present in donkey milk gently exfoliate the surface layer of dead skin cells, revealing fresher, more radiant skin beneath. Simultaneously, our proprietary Bright-Complex™ technology penetrates deeper layers to interrupt melanin synthesis at the source.

Unlike harsh chemical brighteners that can damage the skin barrier, our donkey milk base provides essential fatty acids and ceramides that strengthen and protect. Users report not only lighter, more even skin tone but also improved texture, reduced pore visibility, and a natural luminosity that makeup cannot replicate. The non-comedogenic formula makes it suitable for all skin types, including sensitive and acne-prone skin.

Usage Instructions

For optimal results, apply to cleansed face and neck twice daily—morning and evening. Use gentle upward strokes until fully absorbed. Follow with sunscreen during daytime use, as the active brightening ingredients can increase photosensitivity. Each jar provides approximately 60 days of use when applied as directed.

Anti-Aging & Nourishment

Donkey Milk Nourishing Cream

120ml / 120g J6050 / J0055 216 units/carton

Product Philosophy

The pink variant of our Donkey Milk Cream series represents our commitment to age-defying skincare. While the blue formula focuses on brightening, this pink iteration emphasizes deep cellular nourishment and structural skin support. The elegant rose-tinted packaging reflects the romantic, timeless beauty that this cream aims to preserve and enhance.

Formulated specifically for mature skin and those showing early signs of aging, this cream delivers intensive hydration while stimulating collagen synthesis. The unique pink hue of the product itself comes from natural vitamin B12 and astaxanthin extracts—powerful antioxidants that give the cream its distinctive color while providing superior free-radical protection.

Advanced Anti-Aging Technology

Our Nourishing Cream employs a multi-vector approach to aging skin. First, the small molecular structure of donkey milk proteins allows for deep penetration into the dermal layer, where they stimulate fibroblast activity and new collagen formation. This is complemented by our exclusive Peptide-9 Complex, a blend of signal peptides that instruct skin cells to behave as they did in their youth—increasing elasticity, improving density, and reducing the depth of wrinkles.

The formula also addresses hormonal aging, particularly important for women experiencing perimenopause and menopause. Phytoestrogens from natural plant extracts work synergistically with donkey milk's own growth factors to combat the thinning and dryness that often accompany hormonal changes. Clinical trials show a 40% improvement in skin thickness and 35% reduction in wrinkle depth after 8 weeks of use.

Texture & Experience

Unlike heavy anti-aging creams that sit on the skin's surface, our pink formula has a whipped, soufflé-like texture that melts upon contact with body heat. It absorbs completely within 90 seconds, leaving a velvety matte finish that serves as an excellent primer for makeup. The subtle natural fragrance—a blend of rose absolute and sandalwood—provides aromatherapeutic benefits while remaining hypoallergenic.

Donkey Milk Proteins
Peptide-9 Complex
Retinol Microspheres
Astaxanthin Extract

Application Protocol

Best used as the final step in your evening skincare routine, allowing the active ingredients to work during the skin's natural repair cycle. However, it can also be used in the morning for extremely dry skin types. Warm a pearl-sized amount between fingertips before pressing gently into the skin—avoid rubbing, which can stretch delicate facial tissue. For neck application, use upward strokes from collarbone to jawline to counteract gravity's effects.
